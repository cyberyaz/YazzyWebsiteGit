About

Yazzy Simons is a vocalist and composer from the San Francisco Bay Area, now based in Denver. She specializes in Bossa Nova, Swing and modern R&B. Yazzy is currently perusing vocal jazz studies at the University of Denver under the guidance of Tatiana Mayfield, and performs around Denver with her Brazilian jazz duo alongside guitarist Kylan Fermin.
